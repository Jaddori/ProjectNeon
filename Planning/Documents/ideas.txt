-Start as a white color
-Level up your preferred color

-You take damage based on what color you are
-Enemies do the same
-You fire projectiles the same color as you are

-Progression tree for different colors provide different abilities
-Combine colors with your friends

MVP:
-Three players
-Red, Green, Blue
-No classes
-Can't change color
-Enemies change color
-Players can combine colors to defeat enemies
-Everyone can heal, buff, tank, etc
